Valencia City Guide

Hotels

  • The Westin Valencia: A luxurious 5-star hotel offering spacious rooms, a spa, and a rooftop pool with panoramic views.
  • Hotel Dimar: A stylish boutique hotel near the Turia Gardens with modern rooms and a rooftop terrace.
  • NH Valencia Center: A contemporary hotel with a fitness center located near the City of Arts and Sciences.

Hostels

  • Red Nest Hostel: Budget-friendly option in the historic center, offering dormitory rooms and a social atmosphere.
  • Purple Nest Hostel: Known for its friendly staff, organized activities, and comfortable beds in the city center.

Coworking Spaces

  • Wayco: A modern coworking space with high-speed internet, private offices, and a collaborative environment.
  • La Exprimidora: A cozy coworking space in Ruzafa neighborhood, perfect for freelancers looking for a quiet workspace.

Restaurants

  • Central Bar: A trendy tapas bar in Mercado Central offering traditional Spanish dishes and fresh seafood.
  • Ricard Camarena Restaurant: A Michelin-starred restaurant serving contemporary Mediterranean cuisine.
  • Canalla Bistro: A casual dining spot offering fusion cuisine with a modern twist, created by chef Ricard Camarena.

Nightlife

  • Mya: One of Valencia's hottest nightclubs, with multiple dance floors and a large terrace overlooking the City of Arts and Sciences.
  • Radio City: A popular bar and live music venue with a vibrant atmosphere in the heart of Valencia's old town.
  • L'Umbracle: An open-air nightclub located in the gardens of the City of Arts and Sciences, offering stunning views and great music.
