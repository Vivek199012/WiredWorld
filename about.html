Welcome to The Wired Nomad

For Whom?

"Digital nomads", or remote workers, love to travel but they need fast & reliable internet speeds, and likely low latency, in order to do their job (more so if voice/video calls are required). Some remote workers require a VPN in order to mask their location or simply for additional privacy. This means LAN access to the router for a wired ethernet connection may be desired to maximize speeds and minimize latency!

Why?

Digital nomads have many online accommodation booking options to choose from (short, medium, and long-term stays). The Wired Nomad exists to serve as a single search engine and aggregator. A few Chrome extensions have tried to collect surrounding area network speeds and average them, however accuracy varies widely and cannot be depended on. And, who wants to be restricted to a specific browser? A dedicated website is better, especially for mobile devices.

What information is provided?

The Wired Nomad is a search engine and aggregator for digital nomad accommodations. Info on accommodation internet speeds and other network details are crowdsourced from the community. This info includes: Download and Upload speeds, Ping, and sometimes Jitter. Additionally, whether a user experienced any dropouts and LAN port accessibility are provided. A map display helps to find listings for digital nomads around the world.
